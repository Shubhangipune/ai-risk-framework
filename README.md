# AI Risk Assessment Framework
### NIST AI RMF · ISO/IEC 42001 · EU AI Act · UK NCSC · OWASP LLM Top 10

> **Status:** 🚧 Work in progress · Started May 2026 · Target completion June 2026

A 40-control framework for assessing and managing AI risk in enterprise 
deployments, with a worked case study on a UK-regulated insurer deploying 
generative AI.

## Why this exists

Enterprise AI adoption is outpacing AI governance. UK organisations face 
overlapping obligations from the EU AI Act, NCSC's AI Cyber Code of Practice, 
sector regulators (FCA, PRA, ICO), and emerging certification standards 
(ISO/IEC 42001). Most existing AI governance content addresses one standard 
in isolation. This framework integrates them into a single control set that 
a UK GRC team can actually operationalise.

## What this will contain

- **40 controls** across 6 domains: Governance, Data, Model, Deployment, 
  Monitoring, Third-Party Risk
- **Standards mapping** to NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001, EU AI Act, 
  NCSC Secure AI Guidelines, OWASP LLM Top 10
- **Control matrix** (Excel) with applicability, severity, and maturity levels
- **Worked case study** on a UK-regulated insurer deploying a generative AI 
  customer-service assistant, including risk assessment and 90-day treatment plan
- **References** to all source standards and guidance
